## QUESTION TYPES for Fractions (generate {{COUNT}} questions):

Create questions about fraction operations, comparisons, and conversions.

### Types to Include:
1. **Addition/Subtraction** - Adding or subtracting fractions with unlike denominators
2. **Multiplication** - Multiplying fractions and mixed numbers
3. **Division** - Dividing fractions (flip and multiply)
4. **Comparing Fractions** - Ordering or comparing fractions
5. **Mixed Numbers** - Converting between improper fractions and mixed numbers
6. **Fraction of a Quantity** - Finding a fraction of a whole number

### Context Variety:
- Sharing food (pizza, cake, chocolate)
- Measuring ingredients (cooking)
- Distance and length
- Time (fraction of an hour)
- Money and shopping

### Example Scenarios:
- "Mei ate 2/5 of a pizza and Liam ate 1/3. How much pizza did they eat altogether?"
- "A ribbon is 3 3/4 metres long. If 1 1/2 metres is cut off, how much remains?"
- "Which fraction is largest: 3/4, 5/7, or 7/10?"

### CRITICAL RULES:
1. Use denominators that students can work with (2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10, 12)
2. Ensure answers simplify to reasonable fractions
3. Mixed number answers should have proper fractions less than 1
4. Avoid fractions that result in repeating decimals when possible
