### Overall Style:
- Clean, simple line art illustration style

### For DUAL portraits (two people):
- Both characters shown as HEAD AND SHOULDERS (bust portraits)

- Characters facing slightly toward each other or toward viewer
- Names displayed clearly below each portrait
- **NO statements/quotes in the image** - only portraits and names

### For SINGLE portraits (one person):
- Character shown as HEAD AND SHOULDERS (bust portrait)
- Character positioned in center or slightly to one side
- Name displayed clearly below portrait
- **NO statements/quotes in the image** - only portrait and name
